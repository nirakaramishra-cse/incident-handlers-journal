
# References

The following resources were referenced to support the incident response
concepts, methodologies, and tools demonstrated in this project.

---

## Standards and Frameworks

- National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).  
  *Computer Security Incident Handling Guide (SP 800-61 Rev. 2).*  
  Provides guidance on incident response lifecycle, roles, and best practices.

- National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).  
  *Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments (SP 800-30 Rev. 1).*  
  Referenced for understanding risk, threat analysis, and impact evaluation.

---

## Tools and Platforms

- Wireshark — Network protocol analyzer used for packet inspection and traffic analysis.  
  https://www.wireshark.org/

- tcpdump — Command-line packet capture tool for network traffic analysis.  
  https://www.tcpdump.org/

- VirusTotal — Online threat intelligence platform for analyzing files and URLs.  
  https://www.virustotal.com/
